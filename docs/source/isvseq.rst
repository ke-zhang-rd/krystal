========
is vs ==
========

+-------------+--------------------------+
| **operator**| **meaning**              |
+=============+==========================+
| ``==``      | equal                    |
+-------------+--------------------------+
| ``!=``      | not equal                |
+-------------+--------------------------+
| ``is``      | object identity          |
+-------------+--------------------------+
| ``is not``  | negative object identity |
+-------------+--------------------------+
